About StudyForge

StudyForge was founded with a simple mission: help students turn overwhelming lecture materials into structured, exam-ready study guides using AI.

Our Story

StudyForge was created by Jedidiah, a Nigerian developer who saw fellow students struggling with dense PDFs, long YouTube lectures, and messy PowerPoint slides. There had to be a better way to study.

What started as a personal project to solve this problem grew into StudyForge – an AI-powered platform that automatically transforms any course material into structured summaries, flashcards, and practice questions.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves to study smarter, not longer. Our mission is to use AI to reduce the time students spend organizing materials so they can focus on actually understanding and remembering concepts.

Our Values

  • Student-First: Every feature is built with students' needs in mind
  • Simplicity: No prompt engineering required – just paste and go
  • Accessibility: Free tier available so everyone can benefit
  • Continuous Improvement: We're always making StudyForge better
